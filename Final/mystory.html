My Developer Story!

my name isNEZIRA USMAEL

  • I was born and raised in Harar, Ethiopia. As a child, we didn't have a computer at all so I had no idea what exactly computer was.
  • My siblings, our dad, and I migrated to Canada in 2006. In high school, I took basic computer class which was one of the mandatory classes.
  • With no knowledge about computer and how it functions, that computer class just put me in competition. I was trying and trying to type faster as they were or even better.
  • I typed all driving permit questions from the book to the computer, and any book that is availabe around me. In short amount of time, I was as fast typer as my classmates were!
  • When my math teacher voluntarily taught how to make games using computers, I have always used to wonder how they do all those animations, the coding language that looks cool on the computer screen, and build any websites?
  • I started wondering if I will be able to program one day, and since then my interest about computer started to develop.
  • Now, thanks to our wonderful teacher Brenton Strine and all the Teacher Assistants, I am learning how to code to build a website!
  • Now I don't feel like am all oblivious about those pretty codes I used to see on the screen.
  • I am loving this course so much I just want to code all the time! It's very interesting stuff!!
